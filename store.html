Best Milk, Best Roots About Us and Why Us

Satvik Cow’s milk offers pure Cow milk from Indian breed of cows.

What makes us different and preferred is our love towards nature that reflects in our product. Our cows do not live in the farm rather they live in the village with villagers like their family member and live freely and happily in a natural condition. Milk extracted from cows are purely in traditional style. We collect milk from home to home and make it freshly available to your home on the same day. Village is located close to Pune which has only Indian breed of cows.

Satvik is a reflection of our passion for milk. Milk captures the imagination, sparks interest and ideas, adds colour and flavour, and makes us feel fulfilled and happy. We believe in the significant part played by milk, strengthening and protecting the body and mind to live a fuller, more complete life each day. We know that cow’s milk at its best represents the pinnacle of nutrition and health!
